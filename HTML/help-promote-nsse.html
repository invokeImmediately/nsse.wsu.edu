Help Promote NSSE to Seniors and First-Year Students

We need your help to get the word out to WSU seniors and first-year students about NSSE! Instructors, graduate students, staff, and leadership on all WSU campuses play an important role in encouraging students to participate in this biennial survey of student engagement.

These downloadable items will tell you everything you need to know about promoting NSSE at WSU.

NSSE 2021 Dates

For your reference and to help you schedule your own messaging, here are the NSSE 2021 dates when emails will be sent to seniors and first-year students who have not yet completed the survey:

  • March 1
  • March 9
  • March 15
  • March 23
  • March 29
