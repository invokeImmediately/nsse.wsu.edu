Help Promote NSSE

We need your help to get the word out about NSSE! Instructors, graduate students, staff, and leadership on all WSU campuses play an important role in promoting NSSE to students.

These downloadable promotion kits will tell you everything you need to know about promoting NSSE at WSU—from IRB guidelines to talking points—along with flyers, posters, and images.

In-Class Promo

If you teach a course with high enrollments of senior or first-year students, this kit contains everything you need to promote NSSE in your classroom.

  • NSSE Overview
  • NSSE promotion guidelines for instructors
  • Slides to add to your daily announcements
  • Text to post in Blackboard
  • Suggested dates

Clicking on the link below will initiate the download of a Zip file containing all the documents you need.
